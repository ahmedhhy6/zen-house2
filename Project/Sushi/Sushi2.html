• Nigiri

    ingredients

    Sushi rice Slices of raw fish (e.g., salmon, tuna, eel) Wasabi (optional)

  • Maki (Roll Sushi)

    ingredients

    Sushi rice Nori (seaweed sheet) Fillings (e.g., cucumber, avocado, crab, or fish) Optional: sesame seeds or spicy mayo on top

  • Sashimi

    ingredients

    Thinly sliced raw fish (e.g., tuna, salmon, mackerel) Wasabi and soy sauce for dipping

  • Temaki (Hand Rolls)

    ingredients

    Nori (seaweed sheet) Sushi rice Fillings (e.g., fish, avocado, cucumber) Rolled into a cone shape

  • Uramaki (Inside-Out Rolls)

    ingredients

    ushi rice Nori (wrapped inside the rice) Fillings (e.g., crab, avocado, cucumber) Often topped with sesame seeds or fish on the outside
